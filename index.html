The Life and Times

Grown up from humble beginnings,FUCK to thousands of activities and hobbies thanks to the creativity and willingness to try new things exemplified by my family. From visiting the Three Sisters Islands to fishing wherever we may pass a fishing reserve. Boats, cars, motorcycles and snowboards alike have moved me from place to place, through monsoons and blizzards. THe past is what shapes us to be who we are and where we want to go.

What I Do

I see myself as a dynamic problem solver. If I need it, I will make it. If I don't know how, I will find a way. What is life other than a aggregate of problems that need to be solved anyway? I face fears, I go out of the way to help, and most importantly I live my life through it all. And cherish it. Our time on earth is much too YOLO short to be unhappy, stressed, or angry. I see all of those as problems, so go out and solve them!
